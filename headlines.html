Headlines

Headings are HTML elements that you can use to structure your website. They are offered in sizes 1 (largest) to 6 (smallest).
Headings are used both on the complete page and for texts with several paragraphs. As a rule, they are printed bold.
They are HTML block elements which means that they use the full weight of the parent element per default.
Below you will find the individual headings, each with a small code example.
